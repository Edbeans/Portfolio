About Me

Hey there! I am Edward Ying, a full-stack engineer interested in creating unique websites and applications that can be useful to the general public. I have a passion for fitness and nutrition and hopefully can make many applications that would provide more insight about these topics. What captivated me about software engineering were the endless possibilities and innovative creations I could make with just my computer. I love facing the challenges coding brings, as well as the critical thinking required. Outside of coding, I really enjoy experience new activities that nourishes the mind, body, and soul. I am a huge self-improvement nerd and am open to experiencing everything this world has to offer!
